Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

The extension is completely free. There are no charges, subscriptions, or payments — so there is nothing to refund.

1. No Payments, No Refunds

Twitch YouTube Kick Chat Merger is provided free of charge. Every feature is available to everyone at no cost. There are no paid plans, subscriptions, in-app purchases, or recurring charges, and we do not collect payment information or use a payment processor.

Because there is nothing to pay, there is nothing to refund. This page exists only to make that clear.

2. Previous Purchases

Earlier versions of the extension offered a paid "Pro" tier. As of version 1.5.0, all Pro features were unlocked for everyone for free, and the paid tier was discontinued. If you purchased Pro before it became free and have a question about that earlier purchase, please contact us and we'll be glad to help.
