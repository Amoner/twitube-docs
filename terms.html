2. Service Description

Twitch YouTube Kick Chat Merger is a Chrome browser extension that:

  • Merges YouTube, Twitch, and Kick live chat messages into your Twitch Dashboard

  • Offers cross-platform giveaway functionality (Pro feature)
  • Displays real-time chat analytics (Pro feature)

The Extension is designed for personal monitoring only. Per Twitch Terms of Service, combined chats cannot be displayed on public streams through dashboard modification.
